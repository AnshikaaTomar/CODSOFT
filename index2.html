Number Game

This Java-based Number Game challenges players to guess a randomly generated number within five attempts. The game provides feedback on whether the guess is too high or too low, helping the player adjust their guesses. After all the attempts are finished, the game ends and displays the player's score based on the number of correct guesses. If the player wants to go again, it lets you do just that. at the end of each game, you get your score all summed up.
It's a fun and simple way to test your luck and logic while sharpening your number-guessing skills!
